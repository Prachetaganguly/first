Dear Compassionate Supporters,

HarborHeed Community is launching a vital campaign to provide essential gear—shoes, goggles, and gloves—to the hardworking salt collectors of the Rann of Kutch. These dedicated individuals toil under challenging conditions, and it's our collective responsibility to ensure their well-being. We invite you to join hands with us in this humanitarian effort by contributing to our fundraising campaign. Let's make a significant impact on their lives and empower them to work with dignity. Every contribution counts, bringing us one step closer to providing essential support to those who labor in the expansive salt flats. Donate today and be a beacon of support for the HarborHeed Community's initiative to uplift the salt collectors of the Rann of Kutch.
